About Astana Opera

Opened in 2013, the Astana Opera is one of the most magnificent theater buildings in Central Asia. Built in a classical style with Kazakh national motifs, its grandeur and acoustic excellence rival the world's most famous opera houses.

Key Information

  • Main Hall Capacity: 1,250 seats
  • Chamber Hall: 250 seats
  • Architectural Style: Neoclassical with Kazakh ornaments
